Learning

After going to University of South Florida studying architecture and finding out it would take me 7 years to graduate, I decided to make a change. I left college and decided to take a different path, so in March of 2022, I joined Project Innovate Newark, a non-profit organization based out of Newark, New Jersey. With no prior software engineering experience, I've taken a different approach of learning through mentorship and workshops. I primarily started as a CSX Curriculum Developer by creating a UI/UX Submodule using several free browser programs such as Figma, JSFiddle, and GitHub; and coding in HTML and CSS. In June of 2022, I began to advance as a Software Engineer and worked on our public-facing and admin applications. I've been able to make functional changes that advance the consumer's experience using Javascript, React, and CSS. Lastly, in October of 2022, I started servicing in the People department, which consisted of curating content for the interview process for new potential candidates, marketing documentation for job fairs, and being apart of the interview process for the first round. I'm excited to see where this opportunity will take me in the future.
