About Me

Hi! My name is Shane Palmeri and I reside in the Tampa Bay Area. I am currently a Software Engineer and UX Designer. I've taken a non-traditional approach in learning computer science through Project Innovate Newark, with a focus on career development and learning through a mentorship style experience. Project Innovate Newark has given me the opportunity to make an impact as a Software Engineer, People Manager, and the CSX Curriculum Developer. The skills I have accomplished will undoubtedly support long-lasting impacts on my current and professional endeavors. For as long as I can remember, creativity has driven me to create an outlet for myself and have always wanted to work in a field where creativity and design is a priority.
